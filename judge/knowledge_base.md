# HVAC Billing Knowledge Base

## Technician Tiers and Billing Rates
- **Apprentice**: $45 - $60 / hour. Handles simple tasks like filter swaps, cleaning, and assistance.
- **Journeyman**: $65 - $85 / hour. Handles most standard maintenance and repairs.
- **Master**: $90 - $120 / hour. Handles complex diagnostics, system design, and specialized repairs.
  - *Rule*: Master technicians should not be billed for simple preventive maintenance (filter swaps, visual inspections) unless explicitly required by the contract.

## Common Parts and Market Prices
- **Air Filters**: $15 - $50 depending on MERV rating.
- **Capacitors**: $30 - $80.
- **Contactors**: $40 - $100.
- **Refrigerant (R-410A)**: $50 - $150 per lb.

## Service Definitions
- **Preventive Maintenance (PM)**: Regularly scheduled inspection and cleaning. Typically billed at standard rates.
- **Emergency Service**: Unscheduled repairs. Often billed at premium rates (1.5x or 2x).

## Billing Practices
- **LOT Charges**: Charges for miscellaneous small parts (screws, tape, etc.). Should not exceed $50 unless itemized.
- **Fuel Surcharges**: Should be a flat fee, typically under $75.
